How to Play:

  • 🎮 Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move
  • 🍎 Eat fruits to grow longer and earn 10 points
  • ⚠️ Don't hit the walls or yourself!
  • ❤️ You have 3 Lives - each collision costs one life
  • 🦊 Animal changes: Worm → Fish → Hippo → Crocodile
  • 🍊 Fruit changes: Apple → Orange → Strawberry → Banana
  • 📊 Animals & fruits upgrade every 100 points!
  • ⏸️ Press Space to pause/resume
  • 🚀 Game gets harder as you score more!
  • 🔄 Game Over when lives reach 0 - Press R to restart!
